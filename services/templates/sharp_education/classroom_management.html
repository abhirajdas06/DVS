Classroom Management

Rising to the challenge and requirements of the modern classroom, Classroom Management Solutions provide the ability to orchestrate and deliver lesson content, work collaboratively and monitor student PCs, ensuring that complete student attention and focus is maintained at all times.

  • Real-time instruction tools
  • Interactive Student Thumbnails
  • Remote Login and Logoff
  • Internet Monitoring and Control
  • Instant Student Surveys
  • Testing and Quiz Components

Classroom Management Solutions helps educators control the use of technology in a classroom so they may monitor, collaborate and communicate with students more eff ectively.

Why choose us:

Our classroom management solution is action and goal-oriented. To build a positive learning environment; to have a positive impact on the achievement of students. We create an environment that encourages and enhances educational and social-emotional learning We maintain order to maximize the learning of students We promote and develop self-control by providing positive achievement and behaviour Proper management of the classrooms including effective communication of behavioural and academic expectations
