Sharp Signage Solutions

Sharp hast the most extensive line-up of Digital Signage Monitors from screen sizes of 20” to 120”. The Signage Monitors can be deployed across Campuses, within Classrooms and Corridors to dynamically convey messages in real time. Here are some of the Key Signage Solutions apt for your Education Environment

  • Real-time instruction tools
  • Interactive Student Thumbnails
  • Remote Login and Logoff
  • Internet Monitoring and Control
  • Instant Student Surveys
  • Testing and Quiz Components

Sharp Campus Signage

Digital communication in school - Inform students and teaching staff quickly and easily regarding substitutes*, room changes*, important internal school information and events, found items, current weather, and much more – on modern displays in the break hall, social rooms, classrooms, the cafeteria or the entire school premises..

Sharp Classroom - Door Signage

Digital Door Signage - Provide dynamic information on the current class or lecture session inside classrooms to guests, visitors, students, professors through Digital Door Signage. Use modern digital door signs to direct, inform and call by name. There are many more possibilities. E.g. multiple screens mounted in different sites could display all scheduled lessons.
