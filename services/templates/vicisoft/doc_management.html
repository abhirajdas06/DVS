Document Management Solution

Stop dreaming!! Your dream of a paperless office and still access to all your information even easier and quicker than before has arrived. Welcome to the ViciDocs Enterprise Content Management (ECM) solution. ECM is the capturing, management, storage, preservation, and delivery of your business information. Our flexible core electronic content management solution with rich features for data capturing, archiving, managing, indexing and retrieving of your information. The flexible data capturing module makes it possible to extract data from almost every source with integrated digitization, e- mail, Optical Character Recognition (OCR), image to text conversions and scanning technologies. Managing your documents is made easy with the use of intelligent indexing and tagging mechanisms so you will never lose that important document again.
